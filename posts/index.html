Why we need methodology during trading

March 14, 2020
思考
Market is full of emotions: fear, greed, hesitation, etc. Trading is about how to dealing with emotions. And usually, it’s very hard to settle well, because we human are emotional animals. Here are emotions we may face during bear and bull market. * Bear Bull Buy Too fear to buy Greed and buy on top Sell fear and sell on bottom Too greed to sell So we need a operating manual or methodology to help us settle emotions during trading.
